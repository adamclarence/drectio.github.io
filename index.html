User Case
The Motor Insurance Underwritter
The Motor Insurance Underwriter needs understand the changes in Claim profile of accidents, relative to the distance from home since the COVID pandemic, to be able to increase or decrease the premiums to improve renewal rates at the right risk & profit points.
Having defined the business challenge and understood the outcome goals, the Underwriter and Data Scientist worked together to defined the data criteria and created the codeless data workload. In Microsoft Team, the Underwriter scheduled the automated execution of the data workload, at 6PM on Friday, when the weekend-price-status is set. The Underwriter then received a notification to say the data workload was run, and the workload was automatically stored in the Underwriter’s User library. They then presented their findings to the respective business heads on Tuesday.
